Bex Educations is an EdTech content platform built around two surfaces that share
a single content model: a public blog for readers and an admin CMS for editors.
Both are React and Redux applications talking to a modular Node.js REST API, split
by content domain, with role-based access gating who can author and manage content.

Rather than building an in-house streaming stack, video upload and playback are
delegated to the Vimeo API, which handles adaptive-bitrate streaming so playback
adapts to the viewer's connection. On the reader side, a theming engine toggles
light and dark with the preference persisted per user, and tagging, social sharing
and analytics make content discoverable and its engagement measurable.
