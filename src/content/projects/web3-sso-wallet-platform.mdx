This was the shared identity and wallet backbone for a confidential Web3
ecosystem — several independent products that needed to behave like one system.
Rather than each product owning its own login, wallet logic and access rules,
the platform centralizes identity behind **OAuth2 / OpenID Connect**, gates
access on **SumSub** KYC tiers, and exposes a single **NestJS** wallet gateway
that hides chain complexity behind **Viem** and **Account Abstraction**
(Biconomy + Alchemy).

The interesting engineering lives at the seams: keeping KYC-driven access
levels, the Merkle-tree reward module and the graph-structured referral tree
consistent across products that talk to each other over **gRPC** in real time.

The linked case study walks through the SSO and RBAC design, the
Account-Abstraction wallet flow, the Merkle-tree eligibility scheme and how the
services stay synchronized.
