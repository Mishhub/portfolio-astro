PureTax is a tax and accounting reporting backend built for firms that manage many
clients' books at once. The backend, built on Django and DRF, scopes every client's
data into its own tenant, pulls financial information from **QuickBooks Online** on
an automated schedule, and turns it into monthly reports.

The heavy lifting — syncing and report generation — runs on **Redis + Celery** so it
stays off the request path and can be scheduled for the monthly cadence. Each report
can be rendered as **PDF, XLSX or CSV** with visual charts, and the system runs at a
steady volume of **600+ reports per month**.

The linked case study goes deeper into the multi-tenant data model, the QuickBooks
Online sync design, and how Celery drives reliable monthly report generation.
